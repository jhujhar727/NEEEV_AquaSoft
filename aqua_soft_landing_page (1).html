Aqua Soft — Product Details

Aqua Soft is designed to convert hard water into soft water instantly at the tap. Using natural filter media, the device reduces minerals such as calcium and magnesium that cause dryness, hair fall, and poor soap lathering.

How it works

  1. Attach Aqua Soft to any household tap — no plumber is needed.
  2. When water flows, it passes through safe natural filter media inside the device.
  3. The media reduces hardness-causing minerals and balances water for softer feel.
  4. Enjoy softer water for bathing, face washing, and daily household use.

Benefits

  • Reduces hair fall and improves skin softness.
  • Makes soap foam better for cleaner bathing.
  • No electricity, no chemicals — natural filtration.
  • Low cost and easy to install and replace.

Who it helps

Families using borewell or tank water, students in hostels, people with sensitive skin, and anyone who wants improved water quality without expensive systems.

Mission: To make soft water accessible and affordable for everyone, improving everyday health and comfort.
